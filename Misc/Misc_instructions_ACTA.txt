Instructions:
----------------------
1. Open 'ACAT Dashboard' (on the Desktop)
2. Select 'App (QWERTY)'
3 Close 'Talk Window' if you want to use Email or MSWord [Keep the 'Talk Window' if you want text-to-speech]
4. Open Email or MSWord.
4.1 Enable the 'Alphabet' window if it is not visible.
5. Double click 'Enable mouse' (on the Desktop). [regular actions of the mouse will get dissabled. Press 'Esc' key to resume regular mouse button actions.]
6. Starting typing!
